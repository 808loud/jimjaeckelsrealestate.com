Home Equity Advisors

We don’t just market your home, we help you prepare it to stand out in today’s challenging real estate market.

These are challenging times in the real estate market, especially for home sellers.

We at the Equity Builders Group realize this and Jim has strategically aligned himself with other senior service providers for a comprehensive, turnkey program. We are sensitive and compassionate to the challenges faced by those having lived in their homes for so long and are not quite sure how to go about maximizing their situation when going to sell it.

Who are we?

Our team has over 35 years of combined experience providing the products, services and resources to accomplish home seller’s goals. We specialize in homes that need the appropriate updating, conditioning and preparation to be brought up to today’s discriminating buyer’s tastes and expectations.

What do we do?

We work with you to price, prepare, stage, market, negotiate and navigate the home selling process for you to achieve the highest net proceeds with the least amount of market time and inconvenience.

How do we do it?

By using our combined, complementary expertise/experience and unique approach we work together to position you for success by helping you make smart real estate decisions.

Why do we do it?

Simply put … we have a passion for helping people in your situation. We know how you think, what you feel, what your fears and concerns are and quite honestly, feel your pain.
